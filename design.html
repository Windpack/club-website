The engineering behind Windpack's wind turbine — built by our Mechanical and Electrical teams.

Mechanical Team

The Mechanical Team designs, builds, and tests the physical systems that power our wind turbine. From blade design to drivetrain development, this team ensures our turbine performs safely and efficiently.

Electrical Team

The Electrical Team designs, tests, and integrates the electronic and control systems that make our wind turbine come to life. They develop systems to monitor voltage, current, and wind data — ensuring the turbine runs efficiently and safely.
