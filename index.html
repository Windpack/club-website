Our Team

People are the drivers of change. Luckily for us, we've got some of the most driven and passionate students on our team. Amongst our three teams (Site Development, Mechanical, and Electrical), we have talent from all corners of NCSU (and UNC).

Our Story

Windpack represents NC State University in the U.S. Department of Energy's Collegiate Wind Competition, where student teams from across the country design, build, and test innovative wind turbine technology. Our team combines hands-on engineering with business planning and outreach, preparing students to tackle real-world challenges in renewable energy. Through this competition and our outreach efforts, we aim to advance awareness of clean energy and inspire the next generation of leaders in sustainability.

Mission Statement

To design and develop innovative wind turbine technology while inspiring future leaders in renewable energy through competition, collaboration, and community outreach.
