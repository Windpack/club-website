Siting & Dev

NC State's Collegiate Wind Competition team, designing and building innovative wind energy systems.

Our Story

Windpack represents NC State University in the U.S. Department of Energy's Collegiate Wind Competition, where student teams from across the country design, build, and test innovative wind turbine technology. Our team combines hands-on engineering with business planning and outreach, preparing students to tackle real-world challenges in renewable energy. Through this competition and our outreach efforts, including maintaining the team's official website, we aim to advance awareness of clean energy and inspire the next generation of leaders in sustainability.

Mission Statement

To design and develop innovative wind turbine technology while inspiring future leaders in renewable energy through competition, collaboration, and community outreach.

Project Development Team

The Project Development Team handles project planning, outreach, and competition logistics. They focus on siting, environmental impact, and community engagement.
